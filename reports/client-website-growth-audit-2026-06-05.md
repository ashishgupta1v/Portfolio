## 4. Full Flaw List

### 4.1 Critical / High Priority Flaws

1. Lead capture is not yet a full sales system
- Current contact flow is direct but not lifecycle-driven.
- Missing strong qualification, lead scoring, and conversion pipeline visibility.

2. Weak attribution and optimization loop
- Hard to answer: "Which page/channel brings best clients?"
- This slows growth decisions and ad/content ROI.

3. Conversion pathways are under-structured
- Visitors can consume content and leave without guided next-step routes.

4. Anti-spam hardening can be stronger
- Contact route should have robust throttling + anti-spam strategy.

### 4.2 Medium Priority Flaws

1. UX polish issues in live rendering
- Minor typography spacing/visual consistency issues reduce premium perception.

2. Frontend warning noise observed
- Live console warnings indicate avoidable animation/preload inefficiency.

3. Engagement-to-booking path can be tighter
- Good content, but needs stronger qualification and scheduling automation.

4. SEO growth is content-depth heavy but system-light
- Great pages exist; keyword-cluster expansion and systematic publishing are needed.

### 4.3 Lower Priority Flaws

1. Accessibility polish gaps
- Keyboard focus flow and some assistive patterns can be improved.

2. Compliance trust pages can be expanded
- Privacy/terms/data usage clarity increases enterprise confidence.
